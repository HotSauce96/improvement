Hi again, we gonna see the advices in CSAT Metric!



This is planned to get an improve on the CSAT metric, being empathyc and trying to show the players why they love our games, and why they still playing! After all, they always come back to the games to play some matches and win some rewards!


This content is going to give you several advices in general, in how to get the CSAT highly, how to sustain, and how to recover if the month doesn't start in the best way.



All we know that the first days at the month are the most importants, KEEP THAT IN MIND.



  • Always, ALWAYS, read slowfully what we're sending to the player, identyfing coherence, and logic in the message that we are sending.


  • Protect the first days of the every month as a little baby! It sounds funny, but the first days are esential to get and to assure the CSAT, even if those first days we don't make the regular amount of tickets to sustain our CPH, the first days are that much important to look deeply the players requests, look what they are feeling, and trying to be empathyc with them to "care" our baby CSAT the first days of the each month.


  • Really important is to have a skillfully and complete acknowledge about the games that we give service.


  • Personalization some ticket "parts" as opening, ending, approaching, and some other. For example: a nice ending that can bring some good results is: Thank you for playing Clash Royale, from Supercell we wish you the best.


  • Identify cases that have more than 5-6 hours beeing opened, and appreciate their patience, starting the opening being thankful for their patience.


  • Get acknowledge about the game "language" or "Gamer" words as: Counter, Nerf, Buff, OP, Counter, GG, meta are really helpful to identify what is the point of the player query.


  • In gameplay topic, speaks from your experience playing, tell short stories about a play that you got countered, or a push that you really did well, that would creates empathy between you and the player.


  • Being informed about updates and things that enter in the game, to edit your macros and personalize your answers.



  • Always watch out the automations CSAT answers, you might have lucky, you might not.


  • Paraphrase the player's words in your answer; this always is a way to make the player see that we read their message.


  • Last, but not least, have a good energy answering the tickets, if you get your shift bothered, bored or just with your mind in other things, it probably doesn't engage the CSAT amount that you need to achieve the goal!
